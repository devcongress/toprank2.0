A hideout for programmers.

toprank
Our system creates a continuous environment that provides the right mix of freedom and responsibility, support and challenge, necessary for the programmer to significantly improve their craft.
ambition.

We believe opportunity and ambition is everywhere.

What's missing is a conducive environment. Our ambition is to create the missing conducive environment that births new generations of developers solving local problems in the most innovative ways.

A plan for local growth.
We also believe local businesses (and government) are unable to reach their maximum capacity because they are technically under-staffed. As such, our hackers are required to “volunteer” a year of their professional life at a local business or government agency.

requirements

for participation.

We're new, without funding, which means that at the moment we're unable to take care of the unnecessary blockers to ensure that everyone has an equal opportunity to participate. Accept our sincerest apologies. We promise to tackle them one after the other as resources become available to us.
To enrol in TopRank, you should:
be able to participate (no legal or geographic barriers)
have been programming for at least 6 months
have a system that lets you drive yourself to greater heights
be able to financially support yourself during the program

selection process.

1

Apply

The process begins with an online application.

2

Interview

If you are shortlisted, you will be called for an interview.

3

Offer

You will then enrol for a batch you applied for.

TopRank is self-driven. During the duration of the program hackers can work individually, collaboratively, or both on projects that excite them. There will be some instructional classes and assignments, but not enough to take away the freedom required to pursue individual ambitions.
