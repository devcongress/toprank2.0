for participation.

We're new, without funding, which means that at the moment we're unable to take care of the unnecessary blockers to ensure that everyone has an equal opportunity to participate. Accept our sincerest apologies. We promise to tackle them one after the other as resources become available to us.
To enrol in TopRank, you should:
be able to participate (no legal or geographic barriers)
have been programming for at least 6 months
have a system that lets you drive yourself to greater heights
be able to financially support yourself during the program

Admission is open to Ghanaian citizens and residents only. There's a total of 12 spots available per batch. We run 2 batches per year. We open applications for both batches at the same time. Applicants choose which batch they're applying for. We also keep a waiting list, in case accepted participants drop out before start of program. On rare occasions, we may exceed batch size by one or two.

Per batch, half the spots are dedicated to returning participants. We don't expect a lot of uptake here since we try to encourage our previous participants to return as resident experts for their previous projects. Resident experts rework on the projects, allowing them to draw deeper insights, given the extra responsibility they have to guide newer participants.

selection process.

1

Apply

The process begins with an online application.

2

Interview

If you are shortlisted, you will be called for an interview.

Candidates are interviewed by a panel. Candidates that were recommended by previous participants or resident experts automatically qualify for the interview phase. If a candidate performs poorly during the interview and merit a rejection, they are informed during the interview. Otherwise a rejection will most likely be due to batch size constraints. These candidates can choose to be added to the waiting list, or interview for a later batch.

3

Offer

You will then enrol for a batch you applied for.

TopRank is self-driven. During the duration of the program hackers can work individually, collaboratively, or both on projects that excite them. There will be some instructional classes and assignments, but not enough to take away the freedom required to pursue individual ambitions.
